English Research Forum

Scholarly Exchange for Research in English Studies

Scope and Areas of Interest

Proposals are invited for research addressing original questions and offering a clear contribution beyond existing scholarship in fields including, but not limited to:

  • English Linguistics
  • Literature in English
  • Translation Studies (into or out of English)
  • English Language Teaching and Education

Eligibility

Submissions are welcomed from:

  • Faculty members and academic researchers
  • Graduate students
  • Independent scholars working within relevant disciplines

Submission Guidelines

Scholars interested in presenting are kindly requested to submit a proposal through the provided email by April 23rd.

Attendance

In addition to presenters, the forum warmly welcomes students, faculty, and researchers who wish to attend, engage in scholarly discussion, and contribute to a vibrant academic exchange.

Submission Regulations

  1. Accepted participants will deliver 15-minute presentations based on their research-in-progress projects.
  2. Unlike many academic conferences in the region, participation in the forum does not carry an expectation of publication. The primary purpose of the event is scholarly exchange, constructive feedback, and the development of ongoing research.
  3. Papers will be organized into thematically grouped panels of three presenters. Each panel will allocate substantial time for questions, discussion, and collaborative feedback following the presentations.
  4. Proposals should be submitted via email to aEnglishForum2025@auis.edu.krd no later than March 23 for consideration.

Email subject: Paper Proposal: (Your Name)

Proposal Requirements

  • Abstract (250–300 words) – Summary of the research project, including research question, methodology, and expected contribution.
  • Keywords – 3–5 keywords describing the main themes or frameworks.
  • Author Information – Full name, affiliation, and academic or professional position.
  • Short Academic Biography (max 100 words)
  • Contact Information

All submissions should be written in English and submitted as a single PDF or Word document.
